Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Name, email, phone number, and mailing address
  • Social Security Number (for identity verification)
  • Account numbers, balances, and transaction history
  • Device information and IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Open and manage your accounts
  • Process transactions and send statements
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security
  • Improve our services and communicate with you

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., payment processors)
  • Law enforcement (when required by law)
  • Affiliated companies for account management

4. Your Privacy Choices

You can:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)

5. Security Measures

We protect your data with:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • FDIC insurance up to $250,000
