Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account?

Click "Open Account" on the homepage, enter your info, and verify identity. Takes under 3 minutes.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Ally Bank is FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor.

Are there any fees?

No monthly fees, no minimum balance fees, no overdraft fees.

How do I deposit a check?

Use mobile check deposit in the app — snap, upload, done.

What if I forget my PIN?

Click "Forgot PIN?" on login to reset via email.

Can I bank on weekends?

Yes — online banking and support are available 24/7.
